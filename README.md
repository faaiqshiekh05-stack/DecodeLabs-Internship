# 📊 Sales Performance Forensic Dashboard  
### Project 2 | DecodeLabs Internship

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project focuses on **Forensic Data Analysis** of sales performance using Power BI.  
The goal was not just to visualize data, but to **analyze the true financial behavior of the business** using statistical reasoning and business intelligence techniques.

---

## 🧠 Objective

To move beyond basic reporting and implement a **diagnostic dashboard** that:
- Identifies revenue distribution patterns  
- Compares mean vs median for financial accuracy  
- Detects business trends and anomalies  
- Supports data-driven decision-making  

---

## 🔬 Analytical Approach (Forensic Methodology)

A key part of this project was **Mean vs Median Analysis** to understand data distribution:

- **Average (Mean) Revenue:** $1,053.97  
- **Median Revenue:** $823.62  

### 📌 Key Insight:
The dataset is **Right-Skewed**, meaning:
- A small number of high-value transactions ("whales") increase the average  
- The typical customer spends closer to the median value  
- Median provides a more realistic business insight than mean alone  

---

## 📉 Business Insights

- Revenue shows a **decline starting Q4 2024**
- Despite high average order value, **sales volume is decreasing**
- Major impact observed in:
  - 📦 Laptops
  - 🖨️ Printers  

### 🚨 Recommendation:
- Review inventory strategy  
- Strengthen marketing campaigns  
- Focus on volume recovery in declining categories
