📊 Project 2: Sales Performance Forensic Dashboard (Power BI)
🎯 Project Overview

This project focuses on Forensic Data Analysis of sales performance using Power BI.

The goal was not just to visualize data, but to analyze the true financial behavior of the business using statistical reasoning and business intelligence techniques.

🧠 Objective
Identify revenue distribution patterns
Compare Mean vs Median for accuracy
Detect trends and anomalies
Support data-driven decision making
🔬 Analytical Approach (Forensic Methodology)
Mean vs Median Analysis
Average (Mean) Revenue: $1,053.97
Median Revenue: $823.62
📌 Key Insight

The dataset is Right-Skewed, meaning:

A small number of high-value transactions (“whales”) increase the average
Most customers spend closer to the median value
Median is a more realistic representation of business performance
📉 Business Insights
Revenue shows a decline starting Q4 2024
Despite high-value transactions, overall sales volume is decreasing
Major decline observed in:
📦 Laptops
🖨️ Printers
🚨 Recommendations
Improve sales volume recovery strategies
Strengthen marketing campaigns
Focus on declining product categories
Optimize inventory planning
