# 🔬 CORE ANALYSIS (FORENSIC APPROACH)

## 📊 1. REVENUE DISTRIBUTION ANALYSIS

### Key Metrics:
- 💰 Total Revenue: **$1.3M+**
- 📊 Average (Mean) Revenue: **$1,053.97**
- 📉 Median Revenue: **$823.62**

### 📌 INSIGHT:
The dataset is **RIGHT-SKEWED**, meaning:

- A small number of high-value transactions heavily inflate the average  
- Most customers spend significantly less than the mean suggests  
- Median is a more accurate reflection of real customer behavior  

---

## 📉 2. SALES TREND ANALYSIS

### Key Findings:
- 📅 Revenue shows a **declining trend starting Q4 2024**
- 📉 Sales volume is decreasing despite high-value transactions
- ⚠️ Business is becoming dependent on fewer high-value customers

---

## 🛒 3. PRODUCT PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

### Top Performing Categories:
- 💻 Laptops (High Revenue Contribution)  
- 🖨️ Printers (Moderate but declining demand)  
- 📱 Accessories (Stable but low margin contribution)  

### Key Insight:
- Revenue is **concentrated in a few product categories**
- Weak diversification increases business risk

---

## 💳 4. CUSTOMER & TRANSACTION ANALYSIS

- Customers are not evenly distributed in spending behavior  
- A small segment contributes majority of revenue  
- Discount usage impacts profitability structure  
- Payment methods show preference trends affecting conversion  

---

# ⚠️ BUSINESS RISKS IDENTIFIED

- Over-dependence on high-value transactions  
- Declining sales volume trend  
- Weak performance in key product categories  
- Revenue instability due to skewed distribution  

---

# 🚨 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

✔ Diversify product focus beyond top categories  
✔ Improve marketing for declining segments  
✔ Strengthen customer retention strategies  
✔ Reduce dependency on “whale” customers  
✔ Monitor median revenue, not just average
